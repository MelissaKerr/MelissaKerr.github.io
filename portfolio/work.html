ON SCREEN AND PAPER... I MAKE IT HAPPEN

Hi! My name is Melissa Kerr, an award-winning Graphic Designer who likes to have fun with design and create trusting relationships with clients and team members. I love the challenges brought on by new clients. I love experimenting with new color combinations and I love creating the best User Experience both with the web and in print.

VISIT Napa Valley

WEB DESIGN

When we think about Napa Valley, we think of the Good Life. Indulgences for the Mind, Body, & Soul and who doesn't love that! While in Napa, you can refresh your senses, taste some of the most delicious and locally grown foods, drink world-class wines, try a pampering spa or walk through some contemporary art galleries. There are endless possibilities for the visitor to fully get the Napa experience. When the website user lands on the new DMO site, we hope these Napa vibes show through! Large immersive photography and rich textures, colors and details really draw the user in and easily give the user the important information they want as well as the related content that they may be inspired by and want to try!
